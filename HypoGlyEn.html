First-Aid

Hypoglycemia

The crucial steps to do

Call for help

Try getting help from a nearby person or health specialist or the ambulance.

Check for consciousness and the carotid pulse

If the casualty is responsive give him a sugary drink/food and check for blood Glucose level , If not move to the next step

Check for limbs coldness and the blue braclet

Measure blood glucose

The normal range of blood sugar is 70 mg/dL (3.9 mmol/L) and 100 mg/dL (5.6 mmol/L)
and for diabetes patients 70 to 130 mg/dL

Inject glucagon If the blood Glucose is low

Put the casualty in recovery position

When the casualty wakes up give him a sugary food/drink
